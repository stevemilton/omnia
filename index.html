Capabilities

Everything a CIO and CFO needs in one control plane.

PII Detection & Redaction

Every outgoing prompt is scanned for names, emails, national insurance numbers, and financial data. Detected content is stripped before it reaches any AI vendor. You control sensitivity levels.

Immutable Audit Logs

Every AI interaction is timestamped, attributed to a named user, and stored in a tamper-evident log. When a regulator asks, you have a complete, legally defensible record. Exportable to your SIEM.

Budget Caps & Cost Allocation

Set monthly spend limits per department, team, or individual. At 80% you get an alert. At 100% requests are blocked with a clear explanation. CFO-ready dashboards in real time.

Intelligent Model Routing

Route requests to the right model based on cost, performance, and compliance requirements. GPT-4 for complex work. Cheaper models for summarisation. Sensitive data stays on approved vendors only.

Role-Based Access Controls

Define which employees can access which AI models. Junior staff get approved tools. Senior roles unlock more powerful models. Granular controls at user, team, or department level.

Vendor-Neutral Control

OMNIA works across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and private models simultaneously. No vendor can govern their own usage on your behalf. OMNIA is the neutral layer above all of them.
